Step By Step

Every order, every time.
01
Day 1

Design & Order

You use our online customizer to set your text, font, colour, and size. Your order lands in our queue and a craftsperson is assigned within 4 hours.

edit_note
Online Customizer
draw
Vector Design
02
Day 1–2

Proof & Approval

Our designers convert your order into a precise vector proof. We email it to you for approval before a single tube is touched. No surprises.

03
Day 3–5

Glass Bending

Premium borosilicate glass tubing is heated to 1000°C and hand-bent over a steel form to match your exact lettering. Each curve is held until the glass sets.

local_fire_department
1000°C Hand Bending
bolt
LED & Electrode Fitting
04
Day 5–8

LED Fitting & Soldering

LED strips and electrodes are hand-soldered into each tube section. Every joint is tested under a UV lamp before assembly. Zero cold solder. Zero shortcuts.

05
Day 8–10

Backing & Assembly

The finished tubes are mounted to your chosen backing — clear acrylic, black acrylic, raw wood, or wire-only. Each mount point is laser-drilled for perfect alignment.

build
Laser-drilled Assembly
verified
72-Hour Burn Test
06
Day 10–13

QA & Burn Test

Every finished sign runs for 72 continuous hours on our test bench. Brightness, colour accuracy, and heat output are all logged. Only passing signs ship.

07
Day 13–14

Packaging & Shipping

Your sign is packed in custom foam-lined hardcase with a signed card from the craftsperson who built it. Tracked worldwide. Insured to full value.

What We Use

No compromises. Ever.
science

Borosilicate Glass

Thermal-shock resistant. Stays clear for 50,000+ hours. No yellowing, ever.

Premium LED Strip

CRI 95+ LED chips. Consistent colour temperature. 50,000-hour rated lifespan.

Cast Acrylic Backing

6mm cast (not extruded) acrylic. UV-stabilised. Won't warp or discolour in sunlight.

Silicone Wiring

Heat-rated silicone cable throughout. CE & UL certified transformers included with every sign.
